# Fighting Words

**Overview**

Why "Fighting Words"?  If you're new to faith, or have been following Christ for a long time, sometimes you want to quickly remember scriptures based on key areas of focus for you at any point in your life. 

This project was inspired by a sermon series and inspired by our early morning mid-week prayer time as a community. 

My desire is share creative tools, in the moment, with my faith community that are helpful, useful and encouraging. 

This is a labor of love and is as free as the message of the Gospel. Please enjoy. 


**Use & Terms**

Verses are publicly found in any Holy Bible and may be various translations. Please leverage your personal Bible to find your preferred translation.

## Features
You can find the life verses at any moment you need it. Use it to memorize favorite verses grouped by theme or topic. 

### User Guide

- Go to `https://ncc.originstack.io`
- Select your key challenge or need
- "Show verses" to view the scripture cards relevant to your terms

**Alternative #1 - Search**
- Use search bar to find terms within scriptures (one word only)
- "Show verses" to view relevant matches

**Alternative #2 - Custom Verse**
- Enter your favorite verse to save it in your browser session
- Access it when you want using tags or search
